About ParkSmart NJIT

We're solving one of NJIT's most frustrating daily problems — campus parking — using real data, smart predictions, and a simple subscription model.

Our Concept

ParkSmart NJIT is a campus parking intelligence platform built specifically for NJIT commuters. Unlike generic parking apps, we use anonymized NJIT ID scan data and class schedule patterns to predict when spots will open — before you even leave home.

The free tier shows live garage availability. Premium subscribers get wait-time predictions, spot alerts, and priority notifications — helping them plan smarter arrivals and reduce parking stress.

The Problem We Solve

NJIT commuter students waste time circling full garages, especially between 9–11AM when class loads peak. Existing apps like ParkMobile and SpotHero handle payment and city parking — but none predict campus garage turnover. That's the gap ParkSmart fills.

SEO Strategy

Our SEO strategy targets high-intent searches from NJIT commuters. Primary keywords include "NJIT parking," "NJIT garage availability," and "campus parking prediction app." The site uses structured headings, fast-loading pages, and mobile-first design to rank well on Google. Future plans include meta descriptions, a parking tips blog, and backlink partnerships with NJIT student publications.

Mobile Responsiveness

ParkSmart NJIT is built with a mobile-first approach. The dashboard, garage status table, and pricing cards all reflow cleanly on screens under 768px. Buttons and menus are touch-optimized. The site maintains full functionality at all breakpoints.
